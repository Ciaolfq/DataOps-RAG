# Prompt

## BERTopic Topic Merging Prompt

We refined initial clusters by iteratively merging topics: in each iteration, an LLM with chain-of-thought reasoning [1] identified the two most semantically overlapping topics based on top-10 c-TF-IDF keywords and 10 sampled posts, merged them, and generated a new label. This process repeated until no similar topic pairs remained.

**Prompt 1: Iterative Topic Merging**

> You are an expert in DataOps and software engineering, specializing in data pipeline development, orchestration, and operations. 

> Your task is to analyze two topics representing clusters of practitioner discussions from Stack Overflow and GitHub, and determine whether they should be merged based on semantic similarity.
>
> You are given for each topic:
> - Top-10 c-TF-IDF keywords (most representative terms)
> - 10 randomly sampled posts (actual user questions/discussions)
>
> ## Topic A
> Keywords: {topic_a_keywords}
> Sample Posts:
> {topic_a_posts}
>
> ## Topic B
> Keywords: {topic_b_keywords}
> Sample Posts:
> {topic_b_posts}
>
> Please follow chain-of-thought reasoning:
> - **Step 1: Analyze keyword overlap between the two topics.** Identify common keywords, related terms, and any distinctive terms that differentiate the topics.
>
> - **Step 2: Analyze the content of sample posts.** Read through the sample posts and identify:
>   - The core problems users are facing
>   - The types of questions being asked
>   - Recurring patterns or concepts
>
> - **Step 3: Assess semantic similarity.** Based on the keyword and content analysis, determine if these topics:
>   - Represent the same underlying problem domain → MERGE
>   - Are distinct but related → KEEP SEPARATE
>   - Are completely unrelated → KEEP SEPARATE
>
> - **Step 4: Make decision with justification.**
>
> ## Output Format
>
> **Reasoning:** [Provide your step-by-step analysis following Steps 1-3]
>
> **Decision:** [MERGE / KEEP SEPARATE]
>
> If MERGE, provide:
> - **Merged Label:** [A concise 2-4 word label that captures the merged topic]
> - **Rationale:** [Explain why these topics belong together]
>
> If KEEP SEPARATE, provide:
> - **Rationale:** [Explain why these topics should remain distinct]

## Topic Labeling Prompt

After merging, we used the following prompt to generate final labels for each topic category:

**Prompt 2: Naming Categories and Topic**

> You are an expert in DataOps and software engineering. Based on the following information, generate a concise, descriptive label for this problem topic.
>
> **Topic Information:**
> - Top-10 c-TF-IDF Keywords: {keywords}
> - Representative Sample Posts: {sample_posts}
>
> **Requirements:**
> - Label should be 2-4 words
> - Capture the core problem domain
> - Use standard DataOps terminology
> - Be descriptive and meaningful
>
> **Output Format:**
>
> **Label:** [Proposed topic label]
>
> **Rationale:** [Brief explanation of why this label is appropriate]
